About us

Xmas attack is a Christmas themed simultaneous combat game that brings the spirit of Christmas into blockchain and play to earn(P2E) . Xmas attack stands out from the majority of other P2E games because of its focus on two aspects:


1. Using contract variables instead of NFTs: All in-game characters are non-fungible but are not ERC721/BEP721 tokens. By utilizing contract variables, users can experience the game without paying gas fees.
2. Strict control of the economy: The creators of Xmas Attack have seen several successful games lose investors because of the inability of these games to cope with decreasing token price and highly imbalanced economies where there are more tokens are minted than burnt.

More on the economy can be found in the tokenomics section.
